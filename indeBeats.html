Inde Beats

Centralized artist portfolio + Localized ranking (SoundCloud, Youtube, Social Media)

Inde Beats is a music app that helps artists get connected and discovered without being overshadowed in the music industry, bringing local artists and local listeners onto one platform. Inde Beats lets artists create 30 second videos showcasing their talent in a fast and efficient way, allowing music enthusiast to discover new artists by sampling their music.

INDIEBEATS from ABQid on Vimeo.

When designing the app the team and I wanted to create a focus on our rankings page. After all this is what made us different than so many music apps at the time. When designing the page we wanted a place where users could read, learn, and understand what was going on around the music industry. Not only could they see local news but at the international level as well. Most importantly, we wanted the user to be engaged in our mapping system, to have the ability to check out any rankings in any city in the world.
