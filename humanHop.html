HumanHop

VR/AR app to watch live games from all sorts of angles.

HumanHop is a mobile app that allows fans to be immersed into the action by allowing them to watch the athlete’s point of view (POV) in real-time. A key component about HumanHop is the ability to switch between athlete’s during a game and virtually “hop” from player to player to see their respective POV, we allow users to switch human perspectives throughout the game.

Along with the team we decided to pursue a patent via The University of New Mexico that would protect our idea, which was the ability to broadcast between different places and watch their view in real time.

For example, take an NFL game, as Tom Brady has the ball in his hand and is getting ready to throw a pass to his wide receiver we will be able to see Tom Brady’s POV and see the game through his eyes, as he throws it, we will be able to then switch between players and become the wide receiver. We will be able to see certain angles that has never been seen before.
